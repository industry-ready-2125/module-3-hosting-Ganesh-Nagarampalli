Questions?

We've got answers.

  • How does the Spotify Premium trial work?

    The Spotify Premium trial allows users to experience the Premium features for a limited period without any charge. After the trial period ends, users will be automatically charged for the subscription unless they cancel it before the trial period ends.

  • How do I cancel my Premium plan?

    To cancel your Spotify Premium plan, go to your account settings, select the subscription tab, and then choose the option to cancel your subscription. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the cancellation process.

  • How do I change my Premium plan?

    To change your Spotify Premium plan, go to your account settings, select the subscription tab, and then choose the option to change your subscription. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the change process.

  • How do I get a Premium plan?

    To get a Spotify Premium plan, go to the Spotify website, sign in to your account, and then choose the Premium plan that you want. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the purchase process.

  • How do I pay for a Premium plan?

    To pay for a Spotify Premium plan, you can use a credit or debit card, a PayPal account, or a Spotify gift card. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the payment process.
